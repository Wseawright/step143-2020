Welcome to Rideshare

Rideshare App

Welcome to our Rideshare App intended for college students who desire a more streamlined process of finding a carpool ride!

Instructions

  1. After you have logged in using Google, the first step is to edit your account information to be accurate
  2. To post a ride:
    1. Either click "Use GPS", enter starting location address in the query, or manually enter an address
    2. Enter an end location address in the query
    3. Click "Get Route" to display the desired route on the map
    4. Select a date and time for the ride
    5. Select a price per seat and payment method for the ride
    6. Click "Post Ride" to finalize ride data
  3. To join a ride, simply click the join ride button for the desired listing below!
  4. To rate a driver, simply click the rate driver button for the desired driver listing below!
    • Note: You can only rate a driver once
