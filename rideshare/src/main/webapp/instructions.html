Instructions

  1. After you have logged in using Google, the first step is to edit your account information to be accurate
  2. To post a ride:
    1. Either click "Use GPS" to get your current location or enter desired starting location address in the text box
    2. Enter an end location address in the appropriate text box
    3. Click "Get Route" to display the desired route on the map, directions will appear to the right of the map
    4. Select a date and time for the ride
    5. Select a price per seat and payment method for the ride
    6. Click "Post Ride" to finalize ride data
  3. To join a ride, simply click the join ride button for the desired listing below! Your joined rides should appear under the "My Current Rides Section"
  4. Rides you are driving should appear under the "Rides I'm Driving Section"
  5. To rate a driver, simply click the rate driver button for the desired driver listing below! You can also leave a review if you would like.
    • Note: You can only rate a driver once
